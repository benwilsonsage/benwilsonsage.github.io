Air freight

Leda offers fast and efficient air freight transportation worldwide. Our quick transit times are ideal for time-sensitive shipments such as perishable goods, pharmaceuticals, and high-value products.

We offer a range of services, including door-to-door delivery, customs clearance, and insurance options. We also provide specialized handling for oversized or fragile cargo, and temperature-controlled options for perishable items.

Tracking systems are available to monitor the progress of shipments, and real-time updates can be provided to keep customers informed.

While air freight transportation services are typically more expensive than sea or land transportation, they are often the preferred option for urgent shipments due to their speed and reliability.
