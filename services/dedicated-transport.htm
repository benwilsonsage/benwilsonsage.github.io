Dedicated Transport

Leda Transport provide transport solutions on dedicated vehicles to match your needs. Your vehicle arrives at your collection or import point at a time convenient to you. It's solely for your shipment, and takes your load directly to your destination - it's never co-loaded, never transhipped, and never transferred to a different vehicle, so you get the fastest road transport times possible through Europe. It's far safer, and more secure.

We'll put together a dedicated contract carriage solution to meet your business requirements. Our fleet modeling, routing, scheduling and optimisation methods mean we can match your shipment to real time availability, improving efficiency in the supply chain. We'll save miles and cost where we can, increasing your service levels by reducing transit time. You get the highest quality and best value for your business, every time.

We have access to a huge fleet of vehicles, depots and teams positioned across Europe, ready to deliver your time sensitive, fragile and high value loads.

At Leda Transport, our team is as dedicated as our vehicles. John and Rob have years of experience in the transport industry. They personally arrange and track your delivery, giving you updates every step of the way through to on-time distribution and proof of delivery. With complete transparency and peace of mind, the Leda team is like an extension of yours.

Let us take it from here.
