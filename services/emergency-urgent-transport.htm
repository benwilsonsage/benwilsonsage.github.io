Emergency and Urgent Transport

Leda Transport are a specialist provider of urgent, time-critical, same-day, and emergency courier deliveries and haulage. We'll see your vital shipment through - every mile or kilometer, from collection to confirmed delivery, keeping you up-to-date in real time.

We'll get you moving, even in the most difficult business situations. If you're facing supply chain issues, the impacts of adverse weather, or you've been let down by another supplier and need a rapid solution, you can rely on Leda to support your business, and get the job done.

Our emergency and urgent transport services can handle medical deliveries, hazardous goods, and special industrial deliveries.

We often handle monoparcel and multiparce deliveries, with immediate door-to-door delivery, same-day, or next day before 10am, 1pm, or 6pm, rush courier services.

We have access to a huge fleet of vehicles, depots and teams positioned across Europe, meaning we're able to respond rapidly to your emergency and urgent transport needs.

John and Rob have years of experience in the transport industry. They personally arrange and track your delivery, giving you updates every step of the way through to on-time distribution and proof of delivery. With complete transparency and peace of mind, the Leda team is like an extension of yours.
