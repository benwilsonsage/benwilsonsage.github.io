Groupage

Groupage (sometimes called Less than Container Load, or LCL shipping) means combining or consolidating many shipments from different senders together into pallets or bigger loads of cargo for transport. Grouping your goods with others that need to take the same route is more efficient, and reduces costs. Groupage is a great way to keep shipping costs down - it's cheap and practical for freight in the UK and Europe, and worldwide.

Groupage shipments are sent to regions or cities together, then individual freight items are then sent on to their final destinations.

UK, European, and worldwide groupage, by road, air, and sea, and pallet collection and delivery, are our specialty. We use single and double-decked trucks, so most pallets don't have to be double stacked.

We have access to a network of vehicles, depots and teams positioned across Europe, so groupage means frequent, speedy delivery options as well as cost effective prices at Leda Transport. We'll save miles and cost where we can, increasing your service levels by reducing transit time. You get the highest quality and best value for your business, every time.

We keep full control of the entire delivery process - we can schedule delivery for a specific date and time, we'll keep track of your groupage shipment every step of the way, and provide proof of delivery too.
