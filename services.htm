Services

Looking for transport companies in Newcastle? Leda Transport offers services covering the whole of the UK and Europe. Our specialties are:

  • Dedicated Transport - a vehicle solely for your shipment.
  • Express Transport - same-day, next-day, and priority.
  • Urgent and Emergency Transport - special or time-critical.
  • Groupage - combining your goods with others to keep costs down.
  • Sea freight - transportation by ship to or from UK and European ports.
  • Air freight - transportation by aircraft, often for long-distance and time-sensitive shipments.

We also offer...

  • Freight Forwarding
  • Secure Transport
  • Full Loads, Part Loads
  • Fragile Loads
  • Temperature Controlled Transport
  • Overnight Pallet Service
  • Overnight Parcel Service
  • European Road Freight
  • Specialist Transport - Sliding roofs, Tail lifts, Safety rail and Hi Ab available
  • Abnormal Load Transport
  • Hazardous Transport
  • Warehousing
