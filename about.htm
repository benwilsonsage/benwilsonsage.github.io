About Leda Transport

Leda Transport Solutions Ltd are committed to providing the highest quality and best value service in Express, Secure and Dedicated transport in the UK and Europe.

Leda Transport Solutions Ltd specialise in Express, Secure and Dedicated Transportation. We are a private limited company who have built well-established relationships with multiple clients throughout the UK and Europe.

We pride ourselves on providing a high level of service with strong levels of availability and professionalism, this combined with great communication and a friendly approachable style give us the competitive edge that our clients are looking for.

We concentrate our efforts on our clients by building a good reputation with them and encourage open lines of communication.
